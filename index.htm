One of my favourite northern lakes is Missinaibi Lake, which is found in the middle of northeastern Ontario. It is located 1 hour north of Chapleau, 4 hours north of Sault Ste Marie, and 4 hours west of Timmins (as seen in the map above). I have enjoyed many sunrises out on Missinaibi Lake at the start of a fishing day, and many many late sunsets that turn into stunning starry night skies.
