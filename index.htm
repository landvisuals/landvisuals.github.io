The beauty of Northeastern Ontario lakes

Northeastern Ontario has some of the province’s best forested land filled with magnificent moose, brave black bears, and busy beavers. But have you ever experienced the magic of spending summer nights on a northern Ontario lake? Once the weather becomes warmer, many Ontarians make their way to the Far North to enjoy days full of fishing and hiking, and starry nights lit up by campfires.
